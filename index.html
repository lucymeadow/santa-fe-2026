Walk the Plaza & Shop for Turquoise

Shopping

The Inn is two blocks from the Plaza and steps from Canyon Road. Great walking loop:

  1. The Plaza — Heart of Santa Fe. Browse the shops lining the square.
  2. Palace of the Governors Portal — Native artists sell handcrafted turquoise jewelry daily. Most authentic & often best-priced.
  3. Malouf on the Plaza — Curated Native American jewelry; shop by specific turquoise mine.
  4. Santa Fe Goldworks — Fine turquoise-inlaid gold jewelry since 1972.
  5. Keshi – The Zuni Connection (227 Don Gaspar Ave) — Zuni inlay and petit-point jewelry.
  6. Rainbow Man (107 E Palace Ave) — Authentic Native jewelry since 1945.

Santa Fe Farmers Market

Activity

Location: Railyard Market Pavilion, 1607 Paseo de Peralta (at S. Guadalupe St)
Hours: 8am–1pm

One of the best farmers markets in the Southwest — up to 130 local vendors with fresh produce, chile, honey, bread, flowers. Great for breakfast bites (tamales, fresh fruit, pastries). ~15 min walk from the Inn or a quick drive.

9:30am
Drive to Bandelier National Monument

National Monument

Drive: ~1 hour from Santa Fe · 15 Entrance Rd, Los Alamos, NM

Ancient Ancestral Pueblo cliff dwellings and cave homes carved into volcanic rock. The Main Loop Trail (1.2 mi, easy-moderate) is the must-do — walk among ruins and climb ladders into cliff dwellings. Allow 1.5–2 hours.

Visitor Center opens at 9am. During Memorial Day weekend, a free shuttle may run from the Frey Trail lot (every 30 min from 10am). Arrive early. Bring water, sunscreen, hat — elevation ~6,600 ft.
